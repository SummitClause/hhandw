Our experienced legal team is dedicated to fighting for our clients.

Heller, Horowitz & Feit, P.C. was founded in 1979 by Jacob W. Heller, Richard F. Horowitz and Eli Feit.  In the more than forty years of its existence, the Firm has prided itself on providing first rate representation at a very reasonable cost. The two current principals of the Firm, Eli Feit and Stuart A. Blander (who joined the Firm in 1984), carry on that tradition.

Feit and Blander have each argued dozens of appeals in the state and federal courts, have tried numerous jury and non-jury trials and arbitrations, and have earned a reputation as seasoned and savvy litigators, committed to the zealous and effective representation of their clients.
